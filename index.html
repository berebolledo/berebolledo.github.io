RESEARCH

I am currently a research assistant at the Center for Genetics and Genomics of the Universidad del Desarrollo in Chile. I focus on the development of bioinformatic pipelines for the analysis of clinically important genetic variation

I did my Ph.D. with Anton Nekrutenko and Kateryna Makova at Penn State, USA . We studied basic aspects of mitochondrial DNA biology, such as heteroplasmy, size of the germline bottleneck, and evolution. Highlights of my previous research are shown below:
